Retrowave

Retrowave, also known as synthwave or futuresynth, is an electronic music microgenre heavily influenced by the soundtracks of 1980s action, science fiction, and horror films, as well as the decade's art and video games. It specifically recreates the popular styles of the 80s boom period, often featuring a retro-futuristic aesthetic with neon colors and an optimistic vision of the future.

More about Retrowave

Retrowave is more than just a music genre; it’s a portal to a neon-soaked alternate reality where the past and future collide. Born from the nostalgia of 1980s synth music, video games, and retro-futuristic films, Retrowave captures the essence of a world lit by endless sunsets and cyberpunk skylines. Its pulsating synthesizers, heavy drum machines, and shimmering melodies evoke a sense of speed, freedom, and bittersweet longing.
